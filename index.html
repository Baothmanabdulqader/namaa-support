Privacy

Privacy first. Your Namaa data — name, salary, expenses, commitments, occasions, and savings goals — stays on your device and syncs through your own private iCloud (Apple's CloudKit). We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking, and we cannot access your data. It is tied to your Apple ID and seen by no one but you.

Subscription (Namaa+)

Namaa+ is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited items, sync, full insights, widgets, and data export. Subscriptions are managed in your Apple ID settings and can be cancelled anytime.
